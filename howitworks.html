Your Path to a Settled Mind: Simple in Three Steps.

We designed the Mind Settler experience to be clear, respectful, and safe. Your complete journey is structured, ethical, and ready when you are.

1

Reserve Your Time and Space

The 60-Minute Commitment

Your journey begins with a commitment of focused time. Visit the **Book A Session** page to select an available 60-minute time slot, indicating whether you prefer **Online consultation** or our **Offline Studio**.

  • **The Crucial Start:** We prioritize the **Introductory Session**—the focused 60 minutes dedicated to understanding your goals and tailoring the structured guidance ahead.
  • **Submission:** Once your slot is submitted, its status is automatically set to **'Pending Confirmation'**.
2

Trust, Payment, and Final Confirmation

Securing Your Spot with Manual Confirmation

To maintain simplicity, security, and ethical focus, we use a transparent, **manual confirmation** process.

  • **Payment Details:** Immediately after submission, you will receive clear instructions with our **UPI ID or cash payment details**.
  • **Securing Your Slot:** The session is officially confirmed only after we verify the payment. You will receive a **'Confirmed'** status notification and an option to **add the session directly to your Google Calendar**.
3

The Session, Completion, and Next Steps

Structured Guidance and Progressive Clarity

This step covers the session itself and how we conclude the commitment, completing the user journey loop.

  • **The Session:** Attend your scheduled, structured **60-minute session**. This is a **non-judgemental space** dedicated to awareness and self-understanding.
  • **Session Completion:** Your professional will help you pause, reflect on the session's insights, and identify any practical takeaways.
  • **Ongoing Journey:** You can then schedule your next session, continuing your path toward long-term emotional safety and well-being.
