Confidentiality Policy

Your Safe Space

Everything shared within a session is strictly confidential and will not be shared with anyone without your written consent.

Exceptions

There are legal and ethical limits to confidentiality, including:

  • If there is an imminent risk of harm to yourself or others.
  • In cases of suspected child abuse or elder abuse.
  • If required by a court of law.

In such rare cases, we will always attempt to discuss this with you first whenever possible.
